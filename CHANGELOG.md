# Changelog

## 1.0 — 2026-05-25

Libella ships with 15 units covering the full arc from LLM fundamentals to production patterns — enough to scope, build, and ship an LLM feature responsibly. The final 5 units are reserved on purpose: we're holding them open so the closed beta tells us which production-operations topics matter most to real PMs, instead of guessing. Your feedback during beta directly decides what they cover.
