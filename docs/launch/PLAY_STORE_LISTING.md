## Full description  *(≤ 4000 chars)*

```
Your team has started making decisions with AI in the room. They're looking to you to tell them which calls to trust, which to question, and where the model is quietly wrong. Perpenda is built to get you there — not by teaching you to write prompts, but by training the judgment those decisions actually require.

Each unit is one tight loop:

• Read the bite. A short, concrete brief on how a real LLM behavior plays out in a product decision. No jargon walls, no 40-minute videos.

• Make the call. You answer a decision prompt in your own words — the kind of judgment you'd give in a real review.

• Get calibrated. Your answer is graded against the unit's rubric, criterion by criterion: what you met, what you missed, and why. No vanity score — just an honest read on where your reasoning holds up and where it doesn't.

Then it comes back. Units you've worked resurface on a spaced-review schedule, so the judgment sticks instead of evaporating after one session.

WHY PERPENDA IS DIFFERENT

Most "AI for PMs" material teaches tool tricks that are stale in a month. Perpenda trains the durable part: reading a model's output critically, knowing its failure modes, and making the trade-off call your team is waiting on. The name is Latin — perpendere, "to weigh carefully." That's the whole product.

• Decision-first, not tutorial-first.
• Per-criterion feedback, never a single mushy grade.
• Spaced review built in.
• Calm, readable, no notification spam.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

No ads. No trackers. No analytics SDK. The app talks only to its own backend, over HTTPS. You can delete your account and all your data from inside the app at any time.

Perpenda is in active development. Sign in to save your progress across devices, or browse the glossary without an account.
```
