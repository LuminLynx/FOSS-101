Delete your Libella account

Libella is an Android learning app. Your account is identified by your email address and holds your learning data only.

How to request deletion

In the app: open Settings → Delete account. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data immediately.

Without the app: email joaoport@pm.me from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We confirm ownership and delete within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account record (email, password hash, display name).
  • Your learning data — unit completions, grades, and spaced-review schedule.
  • Transient authentication rate-limit records tied to your email.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged on the normal backup-rotation cycle (within 30 days). We do not sell personal data or share it with third parties for advertising.
